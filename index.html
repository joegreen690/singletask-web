Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you.

Single Task was built with privacy as a core principle. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account creation required.
  • No tracking or analytics.
  • All task data stays locally on your device.

If you delete the app, all your data is permanently removed from your device. We have no way to recover it because we never see it.
